🧑‍💻💻INTRODUCTION TO ARTIFICIAL INTELIGENT


🔥Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. These processes include learning, reasoning,
problem-solving, perception, and language understanding.



  • Learning: The ability to improve performance based on data or experience

  • Reasoning: Using logic to solve problems and make decisions

  • Problem-solving: Finding solutions to complex challenges

  • Perception: Interpreting sensory input (visual, auditory, etc.)

  • Language understanding: Comprehending and generating human language


👉AI is a branch of computer science dedicated to creating systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and translation between languages.



🔑Key Characteristics

  • Adaptability: AI systems can adjust to new inputs and learn from experience

  • Autonomy: Can operate without constant human intervention

  • Pattern recognition: Identifies patterns in large datasets

  • Prediction: Makes forecasts based on historical data

  • Automation: Performs repetitive tasks efficiently
